3.3 PRODUCING THE COMPONENTS WITH FDM 3D PRINTER

3D printing technology

Use your own FDM 3d printer or refer to a 3d printing service in your city or online to get the printed parts.

 Other 3D printing technologies could be used, SLS technology will acquire maximum resistance to the components, but for a hire cost, no color choice(white is the only possibility, otherwise coloring procedure is possible), long processing time and difficult post processing finishing(getting rid or the powder covering every stamp).

Material

The more resistant will be the chosen material the more durable model you will obtain. The model was tested with Polyamide, most affordable and commonly used material for FDM technology, and don't forget to pick your preferred color!
